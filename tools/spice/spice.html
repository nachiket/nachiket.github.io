SPICE Model-Evaluation

We can implement the non-linear device equations in SPICE using different compute substrates: FPGAs or other parallel architectures such as Multi-cores, GPUs. At the moment, I am compiling to these different backends from open-source Verilog-AMS descriptions of the device computations.
