Caltech Memories -- After several official hurdles, I managed to go work with Xilinx in summer 2005. Xilinx has a beautiful office in Longmont near Denver in Colorado. This was my pretty home in Prospect (a trendy Longmont locality). Each morning, I could see the breathtakingly beautiful Rocky Mountains. And each evening, there was a small storm-front bringing lightning, thunder and some rain after a hot, dry day in the office. It was sheer nirvana! (August 2005)
